Who Is Kris Siddiqi? Wikipedia 

Kris Siddiqi is a comedian and actor from Canada.

He is most known for co-creating and co-starring in the web series Bit Playas with Nigel Downer, for which they received the Canadian Screen Award for Best Writing in a Web Program or Series at the 9th Canadian Screen Awards in 2021.

He was also nominated for Best Lead Performance in a Web Series or Program.

He is also a co-writer of the acclaimed CBCGem series BIT PLAYAS, a regular on CBC Radio’s BECAUSE of NEWS, and a voice actor in children’s animations like HERO ELEMENTARY.

He is a former member of The Second City’s Toronto group.

He is well-known for his roles in Bit Playas (2019), Molly’s Game (2017), and The Boys (2019).

Who Is Kris Siddiqi Wife?

Kris Siddiqi is married to his wife, Aurora Browne.

She is a Canadian actress and comedian best known for co-creating and starring in the sketch comedy series Baroness von Sketch Show. She was born in Thunder Bay, Ontario, and now lives in Toronto.

She was nominated for the Tim Sims Encouragement Fund Award in 2000. She was also employed by Toronto’s The Second City comedy company that year.

She co-wrote and acted in four Mainstage revues while there, including “Family Circus Maximus,” “Psychedelicatessen,” “Insanity Fair,” and “The Bush League of Justice.”

She co-starred in four seasons of Comedy Inc., a CTV/Comedy Network production. She appeared as a guest star in the second season of Corner Gas. She was named Best Female Improviser at the 2008 Canadian Comedy Awards.
