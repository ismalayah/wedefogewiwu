Bajan Canadian Lost Everything: What Happened To The YouTuber?

Posted by Jenniffer Sheldon on Sunday, May 19, 2024

Bajan Canadian has made a comeback after disappearing from the Minecraft YouTube community. Here’s what happened to the YouTuber in these past few years.

Bajan Canadian was one of the first popular Minecraft content creators when he started streaming in the early 2010s. 

Bajan was a frequent contributor and member of Team Crafted before he disappeared from YouTube in 2017. It was a group popular among Minecraft fans that was formed back in 2012. He was inactive on YouTube for a few years and has finally made a comeback after his disappearance.

Who Is Bajan Canadian? Meet The YouTube

Mitch aka Bajan Canadian is a Minecraft Youtuber who has made a comeback after vanishing from social media in 2019. Bajan has a massive following and has made his rebound again in June this year.

Bajan Canadian Lost Everything: What Happened To The YouTuber?

Bajan Canadian vanished from YouTube as he posted his last video on 24 December 2017 before making his rebound this year. Here’s what happened to the YouTuber in these three years.

The reason behind his disappearance has been linked with his rumored feud with fellow Minecraft streamer SkyDoesMinecraft. Mitch stopped uploading videos in 2019 and even stopped using social media. However, he is back again with Minecraft content and his fans who have been waiting patiently are more than happy to watch their favorite streamer.

When Mitch left streaming, he was in his prime and had more than 5 million subscribers with millions of views. Now, he is back with his content and has been frequently tweeting and sharing on other social media platforms as well.
