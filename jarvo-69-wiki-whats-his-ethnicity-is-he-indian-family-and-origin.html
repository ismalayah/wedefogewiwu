Is Jarvo 69 of Indian origin? Nationality

Jarvo 69, the actual name Daniel Jarvis, is not an Indian. He is a British national who resides in London. Jarvo professes to support the Indian cricket team and often wears their shirt when he enters the field of play. He also claims to be a member of the Indian team and has attempted to join them in the locker room. Jarvis is a YouTuber and social media influencer with over 180,000 followers on his channel. On his YouTube account, he broadcasts recordings of his pitch invasions and other pranks. He claims he does it for pleasure and enjoyment, but he has faced legal ramifications for his conduct. Jarvis first appeared on the cricket pitch in 2021, during India’s second Test match against England at Lord’s.

He raced onto the ground, dressed in an India shirt, and attempted to bowl a delivery before being hauled away by security. He pulled the same ruse in the third Test at Headingley, coming out with pads and a helmet and attempting to bat. For serious trespassing, he was arrested and punished. He was barred from visiting any cricket venue in England, but he got into India’s first World Cup match against Australia in Chennai in 2023. He entered the stadium just as the game was about to start and went near Virat Kohli, who had to take him out with the authorities.
