Why is my SiriusXM radio not working?

Make sure all of the cables between your SiriusXM radio and your stereo system are connected firmly and securely. Make sure the FM mode in the SiriusXM Radio is turned Off. Make sure host radio is on auxiliary input mode. If the problem persists, it may be interference from another FM channel.

Why does my Sirius radio say no signal?

It may be either broken or loose speaker wires in the door. If the XM Radio fuse is okay and the radio is receiving power and using a power amp, make sure and check the amp fuse. Check the antennae to ensure it is secure or even replacing, which may resolve your XM Radio problems.

How can I boost my Sirius satellite radio signal?

Let’s try the easiest fix first, which is turning off your SiriusXM radio head unit, waiting 10-30 seconds, and then turning it back on. This will restart the antenna and sometimes correct the issue. If the error message persists, you may need a new antenna.

How do I set my satellite radio?

Just turn your radio on with the antenna facing skyward and tune to channel 184 on a Sirius radio or channel 1 on an XM radio. (If your radio is in your vehicle, please make sure it isn’t in the garage.) Once you’re ready, press the button below and service should be activated within 15 minutes.

How do I get to channel 0 on my radio?

Tune to channel 0. Simultaneously press and hold the AUX and Preset 1 buttons. Simultaneously press and hold the Sirius and Preset 1 buttons.
